Diaval is a method that uses machine learning algorithms to predict the prices of various types of gemstones, including diamonds. In this application, data about diamonds, such as size, color, and quality, is used to train a machine learning model, which is then used to predict future diamond prices. This allows for more accurate diamond price predictions, helping diamond businesses make strategic decisions about pricing and procurement.
